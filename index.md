  <h2 style="text-align: left;">Turning Data into Useful Information </h2>
  <p style="text-align: left;"> Most companies want to better understand their operations. The first step is collecting data, and companies tend to generate a lot of data. Often times that data is not used to its fullest potential. One way to get the full value of  your data is by implementing tools to turn that data into useful information, helping you make better decisions to improve your bottom line. We have helped companies in a variety of industries use information to make better decisions, reduce costs, and increase revenue and productivity.</p>


<h4 style="text-align: left;">Integrated</h4>
<p style="text-align: left;">As information flows from the shop floor to the top floor it is adapted to  provide the most value at each level of an organization. People should see what is most valuable to them with the option to drill down to the details as needed. In an integrated facility, information can be adapted automatically, saving time and resources. We have worked with companies in a variety of industries integrating systems including process data, shipping/receiving, QA/QC, and accounting to develop the expert systems needed to optimize operations in today's ever-changing world.</p>

<h2 style="text-align: left;">User Friendly</h2>
  <p style="text-align: left;">In today's world software is everywhere and used for just about everything. Software should make your life easier by reducing complexity with a clean interface. It should be easy to learn and give you access to the information you need, when you need it. We build systems that make it easy to improve your bottom line by giving you the information you need to make better decisions while getting out of your way.</p>

<h4 style="text-align: left;">Simple</h4>
<p style="text-align: left;">Software can be simple and powerful by giving you what you need when you need it. It should adapt to your workflow rather than forcing you to change.</p>

<h4 style="text-align: left;">Powerful</h4>
<p style="text-align: left;">Software is powerful when it gives you the ability to synthesize complex information into straightforward concepts. Powerful is showing people the information they need to see in the way they need to see it.</p>

<h4 style="text-align: left;">Better</h4>
<p style="text-align: left;">Software is better when you want to use it. When it is designed around you and your needs it enables you to be more effective in your role.</p>
